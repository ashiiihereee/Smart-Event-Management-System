Inter-College Football Championship

Where passion meets performance

📅 5 April 2026 👥 Team (11+3) 💰 ₹1000 / team 5 Slots Left

About the Event

The Inter-College Football Championship brings together top teams from various colleges to compete for glory, sportsmanship, and pride. This tournament promotes teamwork, discipline, and the competitive spirit of college sports.

Rules

  • Each team must have 11 players + 3 substitutes.
  • Standard FIFA rules will be followed throughout.
  • Red card results in immediate suspension from the tournament.
  • Referee's decision is final and cannot be challenged.
  • Teams must report 30 minutes before match time.

Requirements

  • College ID mandatory for all players.
  • Proper sports kit and studs required.
  • Team captain must carry the registration confirmation.
