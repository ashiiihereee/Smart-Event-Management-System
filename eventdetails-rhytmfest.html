About Rhythm Fest

Rhythm Fest is the cultural heartbeat of NMIMS, celebrating creativity through dance, music, and drama. Participants showcase talent, emotion, and storytelling on a grand open-air stage. This is the biggest cultural event of the semester.

Rules & Requirements

  • Solo and group performances both allowed.
  • Time limit: 4–6 minutes per performance.
  • Props allowed — to be arranged by participants.
  • Obscenity or offensive content leads to disqualification.
  • Participants must report 1 hour before event time.
  • College ID mandatory.
