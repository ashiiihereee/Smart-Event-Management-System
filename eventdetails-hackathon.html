HackNova 2026

24-Hour National Level Hackathon

📅 12 March 2026 👥 Team Event 💰 ₹500 / team 12 Slots Left

About the Event

HackNova 2026 is a 24-hour national-level hackathon where developers, designers, and innovators collaborate to build impactful technology solutions for real-world problems. This is your chance to innovate, collaborate, and compete with the best minds.

Rules

  • Teams must have 2–4 members.
  • Only original ideas are allowed — no pre-built projects.
  • Plagiarism results in immediate disqualification.
  • All development must happen during the event window.
  • Judge's decision is final.

Requirements

  • Laptop and valid college ID required for all members.
  • Teams must report 30 minutes before start time.
  • High-speed internet access will be provided.
